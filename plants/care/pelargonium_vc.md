# Pelargonium 'Vancouver Centennial' (Stellar Geranium)
## Sunlight
Filtered/Partial Sun.

## Water
Let dry between watersing.

## Food
Once a year in Spring.

## Soil
pH: 6 to 7.

## Temperature
Hardiness: 10

## Humidity
Dry.

## Pot/Repotting
Every few years.

## Toxicity
Toxic.

## Problems
Symptom: brown leaves. Cause: Too much sun.
