# Kalanchoe
## Sunlight
Bright indirect/filtered light or partial sun. Too much direct sun can burn the plant.

## Water
Water when fully dry.

## Food
Monthly during growing season. Stop in late fall and winter.

## Soil
Well draining, succulent/cactus mix.
