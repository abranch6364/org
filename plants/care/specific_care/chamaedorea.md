# Chamaedorea (Bamboo Palm)
## Sunlight
Indirect light

## Water
Water when top 1/3 to 1/2 of soil is dry.

## Food
Monthly

## Soil
Well draining, rich.

pH: ...

## Temperature
~65-80 degrees

Hardiness: 10-11

## Humidity
High.

## Pot/Repotting
Repot when root bound. Does not need it frequently.

## Pruning
Remove dead or yellowing leaves.

## Toxicity
Non-Toxic

## Problems
Symptom: Buggies on underside of leaves. Cause: Mites
