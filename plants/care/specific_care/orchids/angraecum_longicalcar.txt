# Angraecum longicalcar

## Sunlight
Part Sun, Full Sun

## Water
Slightly Dry between waterings.

## Food
Normal

## Medium
Coarse potting mix. Potentially use a non-degrading material like lava rock to limit repotting.

## Temperature
60-85 degrees in growing phase

## Humidity
65-80%

## Pot/Repotting
Limit repotting to prevent distrubing roots.

## Toxicity
???

## Problems
Dislikes disturbed roots. Can set back the plant year.
